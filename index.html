    Gender Equity Alliance

    ABOUT

    Gender Equity Alliance is an organisation which aims to solve gender ineaquality by promoting equal pay, providing equal opprotunities, and creating a safe work environment for all genders.The organisation majors by implementing the following ways.

    • Healthcare services
    • Mentorship Programmes
    • Financial Assistance
    • Promote positive representations of women in media
    • Promote women's leadership

    Our Services

    Education

    Encourage ladies to pursue education, especially in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) field.

    Mentorship

    Gender Equity Alliance mentors women and exposes them to various opportunities that will enable them to stand out for themselves and explore more.

    Healthcare

    Empowering women to take control of their health by providing health insuarance to women.

    Business

    Support and promote women-owned businesses by providing financial assistance and advising them on more opportunities.

    Finances

    The organisation empowers women economically by granting loans to women to enable them to cater for their needs.

    24/7 Support

    GED is working to ensure that support is offered to women at all times.
